Who are we?

Welcome to Minerva's Campus Event Registration System, your number one online campus event registration system in West Africa. We are a digital platform that streamlines the process of organizing, managing, and participating in campus events such as workshops, seminars, sports tournaments, and cultural activities. It enables students, staff, and event organizers to easily register for, track, and plan events, fostering community engagement and efficient event management.

Our Services

Workshops

We specialize in organizing skill-enhancing workshops for students and staff. These workshops focus on technology, communication skills, and personal development, providing a great opportunity for learning and growth.

Seminars

Engage in insightful seminars with industry experts and academic professionals. Our seminars cover various topics from career development to advanced research techniques, aimed at expanding knowledge and fostering academic growth.

Club Activities

Participate in vibrant club activities and cultural events. From music performances to art exhibitions, our platform brings together students who share common interests and passions, fostering a sense of community and creativity.
